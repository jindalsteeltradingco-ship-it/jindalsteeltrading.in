About Us

Jindal Steel Trading Co. is a leading wholesaler and supplier of premium iron and steel products, founded in 2002. With over two decades of expertise, we specialize in MS steel pipes, Angles, Channels, Flat Plates, Metro Sheets, and a wide range of other iron goods. Based in Gurgaon, we serve customers across India, providing reliable, high-quality materials for various industries, including construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure. Our company is known for offering the best rates in the market without compromising on quality. With a commitment to customer satisfaction, we ensure timely deliveries, consistent quality, and personalized service.
